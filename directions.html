Step by Step How To Make Cereal

Steps 1-6:

  1. Grab your favorite brand of cereal from home or store
  2. GET SOME MILK
  3. Get a bowl and spoon
  4. Pour cereal into bowl
  5. Pour milk into bowl over cereal
  6. Place spoon, stir if that's your thing, and enjoy with good vibes only

Nutritional Value (rough estimate):

Cereal Brand Percentage of Good Vibes
Apple Jacks 150%
Fruit Loops 70%
Cinammon Toast Crunch 130%
Captain Crunch JUST AWFUL (AVOID)
Frosted Flakes 0%
